Pakistan has the fifth largest 
population (207.77 million) in the world.
 Pakistan has a total land area of 880,940 km2
 (340,130 sq mi) (including the 
Pakistani-administered territories of 
Azad Kashmir and Gilgit Baltistan). 
This makes Pakistan the 34th largest 
country in the world. Pakistan has the
 seventh largest army in the world. 
The capital of Pakistan is Islamabad.
 Before 1960, it was Karachi, which is 
now the country's largest city.
